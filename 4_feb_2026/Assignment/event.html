Event Details

"Aarambh" is a significant inaugural event organized by the Angadi Institute of Technology and Management, specifically designed to welcome and orient freshers into the college community. Functioning as the launchpad for the Association of First Year Students, the event typically blends formal academic induction with vibrant cultural festivities. It serves as a platform where new students are introduced to the institution's values and faculty dignitaries, while also being encouraged to showcase their own talents through various cultural performances and interactive sessions, marking the beginning of their technical and professional journey.
